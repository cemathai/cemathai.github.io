About me

Designing with purpose,
building with empathy.

Hi, I'm Cecil — a UX designer who sits at the intersection of research, strategy, and craft. I believe the best digital experiences come from deeply understanding the people who use them, not just the problems they face.

Over my career I've worked with startups and established companies alike, helping teams move from messy ambiguity to clear, validated design decisions. My process is collaborative by nature — I work best when I'm embedded in cross-functional teams, asking the questions nobody else is asking.

Whether I'm running user research, building out a full design system, or sweating the details on a final prototype, I bring the same level of care and rigor. Good UX isn't a phase — it's a practice.
